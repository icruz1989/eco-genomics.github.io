About Me

I am an evolutionary biologist specializing in how plants respond to biotic and abiotic stressors, combining tools from genomics, bioinformatics, and evolutionary ecology. My research seeks to understand plant adaptation in the context of climate change, using both experimental and computational approaches.

Research Interests

  • Plant-herbivore interactions
  • Ecological genomics
  • Quantitative and chemical ecology
  • Climate adaptation in plant populations
